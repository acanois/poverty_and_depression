Effects of Poverty on Depression

For this project, I tried to find a correlation between depression and various socio-economic factors. Ultimately, I discovered that it's very difficult to find a correlation between depression and anything. I went through several different risk factors before settling on poverty level, which had the highest correlation at only about 0.3. Employment status, whether employed or not, yielded almost no correlation. This was the interesting part to me. I thought that depression would be strongly correlated to at least one of these factors, but it wasn't.
